About NxR Esports

NxR Esports is a competitive gaming organisation focused on building elite teams in major esports titles including BGMI, Valorant, and upcoming global esports tournaments. Our mission is to represent India on the international esports stage while building a powerful gaming community, professional players, and world-class content creators.


Founded with the goal of becoming a Tier-1 esports organisation, NxR focuses on competitive excellence, creator collaborations, and global esports expansion.
